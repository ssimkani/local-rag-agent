# After Action Report — Fall Recruiting Fair, 18 September 2025

## Event Summary

Det 999 staffed a recruiting table at the Midstate University Fall Activities Fair, 18 September 2025, 1000-1500 in the Student Union. Six cadets on rotation plus TSgt Romano.

## Results

- 47 student interactions logged.
- 21 prospective students added to the interest list.
- 8 attended the follow-up info session the following week.

## What Went Well

- The OCP-uniformed presence drew strong attention from students.
- C/Amn Pat Williams and C/Amn Skylar Bennett, both AS100 cadets, were extremely effective at peer-level conversations.
- The QR-coded interest form (built by the KM Officer the week prior) captured contacts cleanly and was a notable improvement over the previous paper sign-up.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Materials Shortage

Brochures ran out by 1230. We had brought 50 and needed roughly 75. Recommend ordering 100 for the spring fair.

### Coverage Gap

There was a 20-minute window between 1240 and 1300 where the table was uncovered because two cadet shifts overlapped incorrectly on the rotation sheet. The KM Officer's later review found that the rotation sheet had two different versions in circulation (paralleling the issue noted in `aar_003_dining_out_2025-10-04`).

### Inconsistent Messaging

When asked about the scholarship process, cadets gave varying answers. We need a one-page FAQ that all recruiting cadets can reference.

## Issues for Action

| Issue | Owner | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Brochure quantity | TSgt Romano | Order 100 for spring |
| Single-version rotation sheet | C/Capt Okafor | Apply doc versioning guidance |
| Recruiting FAQ | C/SSgt Sullivan | Draft FAQ, route to Wing Staff |

## Recurring Themes

Document version control is now the **third** recurring issue this semester (see `aar_002` and `aar_003`). The KM Officer is making this a priority.
