# After Action Report — Warrior Day, 17 October 2025

## Event Summary

Semester Warrior Day held at the Midstate University ROTC training field, 0700-1500. Activities: drill evaluation, Warrior Knowledge testing, leadership reaction course, team-building.

OCP uniform per `sop_001_uniform_standards` section 3.2. All 4 flights participated. 72 cadets present.

## Results

### Drill Evaluations (per `sop_006_drill_evaluation`)

| Flight | Score | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Alpha (C/1st Lt Diaz) | 3.2 | Proficient |
| Bravo (C/1st Lt Hayes) | 3.6 | Proficient |
| Charlie (C/2d Lt Park) | 3.4 | Proficient |
| Delta (C/2d Lt Murphy) | 2.8 | Needs improvement |

### Warrior Knowledge Testing

- AS100: 14 of 18 cadets passed all categories on first attempt.
- AS200: 19 of 20 cadets passed.
- AS300/AS400: full proficiency demonstrated.

## What Went Well

- Schedule held. Events ran within ±5 minutes of plan all day.
- Hydration plan, informed by `aar_005_pft_2025-09-30`, was solid. No hydration incidents.
- The leadership reaction course revealed strong AS200 leadership talent — notable performance from C/SSgt Jamie Sullivan.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Delta Flight Drill

Delta Flight scored below the 3.0 threshold and is required to remediate. C/2d Lt Murphy attributes this to inconsistent practice attendance. Mission Support Group CC will work with Delta on a remediation plan before the next evaluation.

### Phonetic Alphabet (Again)

4 AS100 failures in Warrior Knowledge — 3 of them on the phonetic alphabet. This issue was first flagged in `aar_002_llab_2025-09-26` and addressed in `aar_006_llab_2025-10-10`. Marginal improvement, but recommendation now is to make the phonetic alphabet a daily-rep item in each flight's pre-LLAB check.

### Late Arrival of Equipment

Cones for the drill area arrived 15 minutes late; setup was rushed. Lesson: confirm equipment delivery the day before.
