# After Action Report — Det 999 Dining Out, 4 October 2025

## Event Summary

Annual Dining Out held at the Midstate University Conference Center. Attendance: 68 cadets, 5 cadre, 12 guests (alumni and family). Guest speaker: Col (Ret) Janine Hollister, Class of 1998.

Planning followed `opord_003_dining_out_planning`. Event ran from 1800 to 2200.

## What Went Well

- Venue setup completed on time despite vendor delivery delays.
- Mr. Vice (C/Capt Drew Larson) executed the rules of the mess with confidence; guests reported the humor landed well.
- Guest speaker remarks were well-paced and connected to current AS400 career planning.
- Service Dress uniform compliance was high; only two minor discrepancies noted.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Headcount Mismatch

Final RSVP count was 84 but only 80 plated meals were ordered. The catering manager covered the gap, but this required a last-minute scramble. Root cause: the RSVP spreadsheet was updated by multiple people without version control. The KM Officer (C/Capt Sana Okafor) is recommending a single-form intake going forward.

### Late Setup of Head Table

Head table was not fully set until 1745, 15 minutes behind schedule. Cause: the rehearsal Friday afternoon ran short and several setup details were missed.

### Toast Order Confusion

C/Lt Col Morgan Patel (Wing CV) noted that the toast sequence printed in the program differed from the rehearsed order. The program had been re-printed Friday evening but the rehearsal used an earlier version. Mr. Vice adapted in real time.

## Issues for Action

| Issue | Owner | Action |
|---|---|---|
| RSVP system | C/Capt Okafor | Build single-form intake for future events |
| Rehearsal completeness | Wing CV | Mandatory full setup walk-through on day-of |
| Document version control | C/Capt Okafor | Establish doc-versioning convention in #ops-announce |

## Recurring Themes

The "multiple versions of the same document" problem has now appeared in 2 events this semester. The KM Officer is drafting guidance for the Wing Staff.

## Lessons for Future Dining Outs

- Lock RSVP count 7 days out, not 3.
- Conduct a full setup rehearsal, not a partial one.
- Single source of truth for the program.

## Distribution

Wing Staff, Cadre, Knowledge Management Officer.
