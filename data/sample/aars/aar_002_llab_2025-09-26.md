# After Action Report — LLAB 26 September 2025

## Event Summary

Second LLAB of the semester. Theme: drill fundamentals refresh and Warrior Knowledge spot-checks. Total attendance: 71 cadets (3 excused per `sop_004_leave_requests`).

## What Went Well

- Audio system worked after the portable PA was procured following `aar_001_llab_2025-09-12`.
- Drill blocks ran on time. Bravo and Charlie flights showed visible improvement from the previous week.
- Warrior Knowledge spot-checks generated good engagement; AS200 study leads are clearly making an impact.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Uniform Inspection Still Overran

In-ranks inspection ran 19 minutes — better than week 1, but still over the 15-minute target. C/Capt Brooks's interim guidance helped, but flight CCs report the SOP itself needs to be updated. Recommend prioritizing the SOP revision for the next Wing Staff meeting.

This is the **second consecutive AAR** flagging uniform inspection timing. Wing CC has asked the KM Officer (C/Capt Sana Okafor) to track this as an explicit recurring issue.

### PFT Signup Confusion

The upcoming PFT (scheduled 2025-09-30, per `opord_001_fall_semester_2025`) had cadets confused about where to sign up. The signup link was buried in the #ops-announce channel. Two cadets reported they could not find it before the deadline.

Recommendation: pin signup links to channels with a #signups tag. Owner: C/Capt Sana Okafor (KM).

### AS100 Phonetic Alphabet Gaps

Spot-checks showed several AS100 cadets confusing M (Mike) with N (November) and W (Whiskey) with V (Victor). The AS200 study lead has scheduled extra drill sessions.

Uniform inspection time has now been flagged in 2 of 2 AARs this semester. Suggestion: a structural change (not just an SOP edit) may be needed — possibly pre-inspection self-checks by flight before formal in-ranks.
