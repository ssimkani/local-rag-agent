# After Action Report — LLAB 12 September 2025

## Event Summary

First LLAB of AY 25-26. Theme: introduction for AS100s, refresh for returning cadets. All four flights present. Total attendance: 74 cadets.

## What Went Well

- AS100 in-processing went smoothly. New cadets had uniforms issued and were oriented before formation.
- Wing CC (C/Col Alex Reyes) opening remarks set a clear tone for the semester.
- AS200 breakout on FT prep was well-received per post-event feedback.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Uniform Inspection Overran Schedule

The in-ranks inspection ran 22 minutes, well over the 15-minute target set in `sop_003_llab_conduct`. Contributing factors:

- Flight CCs were inspecting each ribbon and nameplate individually rather than scanning for obvious discrepancies.
- Alpha flight had multiple new cadets unfamiliar with the inspection sequence.
- C/1st Lt Casey Diaz noted that the inspection criteria in `sop_001_uniform_standards` could be more explicit about which items are pass/fail vs. spot-correct.

### Audio Failure

The Field House PA system failed during the Wing CC's remarks. Backup plan: none in place. Cadets in the back ranks could not hear announcements. C/Capt Sam Nguyen (PA Officer) recommends a portable speaker as backup, with a request submitted to Mission Support.

### Accountability Delay

Initial accountability took 8 minutes. Several cadets arrived between 1450 and 1500. Per `sop_003_llab_conduct`, arrival NLT 1445 is required.

## Issues for Action

| Issue | Owner | Suggested Action |
|---|---|---|
| Inspection time | C/Capt Brooks | Update `sop_001` with clearer pass/fail criteria |
| Audio backup | C/Capt Nguyen | Procure portable PA |
| Late arrivals | Flight CCs | Reinforce arrival time with each flight |

## Recurring Themes

This is the first AAR of the semester, but the uniform inspection time issue is consistent with prior-year AARs. Recommend the Wing Staff treat this as a tracked recurring issue.

## Distribution

Wing Staff, Cadre, Knowledge Management Officer.
