# After Action Report — Fall PFT, 30 September 2025

## Event Summary

Fall Physical Fitness Test conducted at Memorial Stadium track. Report time 0630, completion 0915. 64 cadets tested. Conducted per `sop_002_pt_test_procedures`.

## Results

- 58 cadets passed.
- 4 cadets failed; placed on a PT improvement plan per `sop_002` section 6.
- 2 cadets unable to test due to medical hold.

## What Went Well

- Sign-in was efficient. Pre-event hydration was reasonable for most cadets.
- C/Capt Brooks coordinated the testing NCOs cleanly.
- The signup pinning recommended in `aar_002_llab_2025-09-26` had been implemented; no signup confusion reported this round.

## What Did Not Go Well

### Two Hydration-Related Incidents

Two cadets needed to stop the 1.5-mile run for hydration support. Neither required medical evacuation, but both reported they had under-hydrated. The PFT SOP places hydration responsibility on the cadet, but pre-event reminders may need to be more emphatic.

### Late Arrivals

3 cadets arrived between 0635 and 0645. The 0630 report time is non-negotiable; suggest flight CCs do a 24-hour reminder via flight chat.

### Scoring Sheet Discrepancy

One cadet's score was initially recorded incorrectly (sit-up count off by 5). Caught and corrected during the same-day review. The scoring sheet still has handwritten entry; recommend transitioning to a digital scoresheet — a project the KM Officer (C/Capt Sana Okafor) is willing to take on.

## Issues for Action

| Issue | Owner | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Hydration reminders | Flight CCs | 24-hour reminder in flight chat |
| Late arrivals | Flight CCs | Reinforce report time |
| Digital scoresheet | C/Capt Okafor | Prototype for spring PFT |

## Recurring Themes

Late arrivals (also noted in `aar_001_llab_2025-09-12`) continue to be a low-grade chronic problem.

## Distribution

Wing Staff, Cadre, Knowledge Management Officer.
