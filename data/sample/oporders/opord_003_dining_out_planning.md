# OPORD — Annual Dining Out Planning

## 1. Situation

Det 999 will host its annual Dining Out on 4 October 2025 at the Midstate University Conference Center. Last year's Dining Out identified several lessons that this OPORD addresses up front.

## 2. Mission

Det 999 conducts a Dining Out on 4 Oct 2025 in honor of the wing and guests, executing the formal mess in accordance with Air Force traditions.

### 3.3 Standards

- Uniform: Service Dress per `sop_001_uniform_standards`.
- Guest dress: business formal.
- No cell phones at table after the President seats the mess.
- Toasts in order printed in the program. President's word is final.

### 3.4 Critical Lessons from Prior Events

The KM Officer (C/Capt Okafor) compiled the following carryover lessons:

- **Single source of truth for the program.** All copies are version-controlled in the #dining-out channel. Old versions deleted, not just renamed.
- **RSVP locked 7 days out.** No additions after 27 Sep.
- **Full rehearsal day-of.** Partial walkthroughs have caused setup misses in past events.
