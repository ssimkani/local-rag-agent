# Det 999 Fall Semester 2025 OPORD

## 1. Situation

Det 999 is executing AY 25-26 with a wing of 74 cadets across 4 flights. Training priorities are FT preparation (AS200), leadership development (AS300/AS400), and onboarding (AS100). Recurring KM goal: implement structural improvements identified in last year's AARs.

## 2. Mission

Det 999 conducts weekly LLAB training, semester PFT evaluation, recruiting outreach, and ceremonial events from 25 Aug to 12 Dec 2025 to produce mission-ready cadets prepared for Field Training and commissioning.

## 3. Execution

### 3.1 Commander's Intent

By end of semester:

- All AS200 FT-bound cadets pass PFT and demonstrate Warrior Knowledge proficiency.
- All flights achieve drill proficiency (≥3.0) at Warrior Day.
- Wing Staff has functional continuity documentation (KM Officer priority).
- Recruiting interest list exceeds 60 prospects.
