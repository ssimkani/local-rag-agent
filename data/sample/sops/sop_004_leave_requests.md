# Leave and Absence Requests

## 1. Purpose

This SOP describes the process for requesting authorized absence from Det 999 training activities, including LLAB, AS class, and PT sessions.

## 2. Scope

Applies to absences from any mandatory training event. Does not apply to medical emergencies, which should be reported via the chain as soon as the cadet is able.

## 3. Request Process

1. Cadet submits a Leave Request form in the Det 999 Teams "Requests" channel at least **72 hours** before the absence.
2. Flight CC reviews and routes to the appropriate Group CC.
3. Group CC approves or denies and notifies the cadet.
4. Approved absences are logged in the Wing Staff continuity tracker.

## 4. Required Information

- Cadet name, AS year, flight.
- Event(s) to be missed.
- Date and time.
- Reason (academic conflict, medical appointment, family emergency, etc.).
- Make-up plan if applicable.

## 5. Approval Authority

| Absence Type | Approval Authority |
|---|---|
| Single training event | Flight CC |
| Multiple consecutive events | Group CC |
| Field Training or AFROTC formal event | Det/CC (Lt Col Whitford) |

## 6. Short-Notice Absences

Absences requested less than 72 hours out require justification and are approved only for emergencies or unavoidable academic conflicts. Repeated short-notice requests trigger a counseling session with the Flight CC.

## 7. No-Shows

Failure to attend without prior approval is recorded as an unexcused absence. Three unexcused absences in a semester trigger a Standards & Evaluations review (C/Capt Taylor Brooks).

## 8. Point of Contact

C/Maj Riley Chen (Mission Support Group CC) owns this process. Questions can also go to the Knowledge Management Officer, C/Capt Sana Okafor.
