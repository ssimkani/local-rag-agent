# Warrior Knowledge Requirements and Study Program

## 1. Purpose

This SOP outlines required Warrior Knowledge for each AS year and the study program supporting Field Training preparation.

## 2. Required Knowledge by AS Year

### AS100
- Air Force Song (verses 1-3).
- Chain of Command from POTUS through Det/CC.
- Code of Conduct, Articles I-VI.
- General Orders 1-3.
- Phonetic alphabet.

### AS200
- All AS100 material.
- Air Force Core Values and operational doctrine basics.
- Major Commands and their primary missions.
- Significant dates in Air Force history.

### AS300 and AS400
- All AS100 and AS200 material.
- Field Training-specific knowledge as published in the FT Prep guide.

## 3. Study Resources

- Warrior Knowledge handbook (provided at AS100 orientation).
- Weekly drill cards published in the #warrior-knowledge channel.
- Peer study groups led by AS200 cadets each Tuesday at 1900.

## 4. Testing

Warrior Knowledge is evaluated:
- Spot-checked during LLAB by flight CCs.
- Formally tested at the Warrior Day event each semester (see `aar_007_warrior_day_2025-10-17`).
- Tested as part of FT prep for AS300 cadets.

## 5. Standards

Cadets are expected to recite required material on demand, in correct form, without hesitation. Failure to demonstrate proficiency results in additional study assignments and follow-up testing.

## 6. Recurring Issues

AAR feedback has identified the following recurring gaps:

- AS100 cadets often master AS100 material in isolation but struggle when questions are asked out of order.
- The phonetic alphabet is frequently confused under pressure.
- Air Force history dates are the most-failed category.

The KM Officer (C/Capt Sana Okafor) and AS200 study leads have proposed weekly mixed-format drill sessions.
