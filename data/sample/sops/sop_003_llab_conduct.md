# Leadership Laboratory (LLAB) Conduct

## 1. Purpose

This SOP standardizes the conduct of weekly Leadership Laboratory (LLAB) sessions at Det 999.

## 2. Schedule

LLAB occurs every Thursday from 1500 to 1700 local time at the Midstate University Field House, unless rescheduled in the weekly OPORD.

## 3. Required Attendance

All AS100, AS200, AS300, and AS400 cadets attend. Absences require prior approval via the Leave Request form (see `sop_004_leave_requests`).

## 4. Uniform

Service Dress per `sop_001_uniform_standards` unless otherwise specified. Cadets are expected to arrive uniform-ready; on-site uniform changes are not authorized.

## 5. Standard Sequence

1. **1445** - Cadets begin arriving and form by flight.
2. **1500** - Formation, accountability, in-ranks inspection.
3. **1520** - Opening remarks from Wing CC (C/Col Alex Reyes).
4. **1530** - Breakout training by AS year group.
5. **1630** - Reconvene as wing; announcements, closing remarks.
6. **1700** - Dismissal.

## 6. In-Ranks Inspection

Flight CCs (C/1st Lt Diaz, C/1st Lt Hayes, C/2d Lt Park, C/2d Lt Murphy) conduct primary inspections. Group CCs spot-check. Discrepancies are recorded and reviewed at the next Wing Staff meeting.

## 7. Lessons Learned

Multiple AARs have flagged the following issues:

- In-ranks inspections regularly run past the 1520 mark, compressing the training block. Flight CCs should target 15 minutes maximum.
- Late arrivals cause accountability delays. Cadets should arrive NLT 1445.
- Public address audio is unreliable in the Field House; have a backup plan.

See `aar_001_llab_2025-09-12` and `aar_002_llab_2025-09-26` for specific examples.

## 8. Point of Contact

C/Capt Drew Larson (S3) is responsible for LLAB planning. C/Maj Jordan Kim (Ops Group CC) executes.
