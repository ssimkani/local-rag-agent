# Uniform Wear and Appearance Standards

## 1. Purpose

This SOP establishes uniform wear and appearance requirements for all cadets assigned to Det 999 during Academic Year 25-26. It supplements AFI 36-2903 and AFROTCI 36-2008.

## 2. Applicability

All AS100, AS200, AS300, and AS400 cadets are required to comply. Cadre may grant exceptions in writing.

## 3. Authorized Uniforms

### 3.1 Service Dress (Blues)
- Worn for LLAB unless the weekly OPORD directs otherwise.
- Worn for the Dining Out and formal events.
- Includes ribbons, nameplate, and rank insignia properly affixed.
- Black oxford shoes, shined to a high gloss.
- White or grey crew-neck undershirt only; no logos visible.

### 3.2 Operational Camouflage Pattern (OCP)
- Worn for Warrior Day and field training events.
- Sleeves down unless directed otherwise in the OPORD.
- Tan boots, polished but not shined.
- OCP cap when outdoors uncovered area.

### 3.3 Physical Training (PT) Uniform
- Issued PT shirt and shorts.
- White or black athletic socks, athletic shoes.
- Worn for the PFT and PT sessions per the weekly OPORD.

## 4. Grooming Standards

Cadets will adhere to AFI 36-2903 standards. Hair, facial hair, and accessories will not exceed regulation. See `ref_001_acronym_glossary` for unfamiliar references.

## 5. Inspection Procedure

Uniform inspections occur during LLAB in-ranks formation. Flight CCs conduct primary inspections; group CCs spot-check. Detailed inspection criteria are in `sop_003_llab_conduct`.

## 6. Common Discrepancies

The following discrepancies have appeared repeatedly in AARs and should be self-checked before LLAB:

- Ribbons misaligned or out of order of precedence.
- Shoes scuffed.
- Hair out of regulation.
- Missing nameplate.
- Service cap not present when required.
