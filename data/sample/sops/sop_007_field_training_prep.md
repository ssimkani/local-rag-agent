# Field Training Preparation Program

## 1. Purpose

This SOP describes the Field Training (FT) preparation program for AS200 cadets selected to attend FT in Summer 2026.

## 2. Selection Status

AS200 cadets pursuing contract receive an FT slot in the spring through national-level selection. Once notified, the cadet enters the Det 999 FT Prep track.

## 3. Program Components

### 3.1 Physical Preparation
- Mandatory PT sessions Monday/Wednesday/Friday 0600-0700.
- Run progression program targeting a 1.5-mile time below 11:00.
- Push-up and sit-up progression per the FT Prep guide.

### 3.2 Knowledge Preparation
- Full Warrior Knowledge mastery per `sop_005_warrior_knowledge`.
- FT-specific knowledge: dorm setup, room arrangement, reporting statements, chain of command.

### 3.3 Drill Preparation
- Additional drill practice with AS300 cadets serving as flight leaders.
- See `sop_006_drill_evaluation` for standards.

### 3.4 Uniform Preparation
- OCP issue and fit check at the Mission Support office.
- Shoe shining and uniform maintenance.

## 4. FT Survivors Program

AS300 cadets who recently returned from FT serve as mentors. Each FT-bound cadet is paired with a survivor. Mentorship pairings are tracked by the KM Officer (C/Capt Sana Okafor).

## 5. Mock Inspections

- Three mock dorm inspections during the spring semester.
- One mock FT week conducted the weekend before FT departure.

## 6. Common Pitfalls

From AAR feedback by AS300 returnees:

- Cadets often under-prepare on dorm setup.
- Reporting statement memorization lags other knowledge.
- The fitness gap between weekly PT and the FT pace is larger than expected; the M/W/F sessions are not optional.

## 7. Point of Contact

C/Maj Jordan Kim (Ops Group CC) and Capt Lin Zhao (AS200 instructor) co-own the program.
